THE SEVENTH CONVOCATION OF THE EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CENTER

Overview of the 7th Convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center(EASTC)

The 7th Convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic istitutuions of gathering together Academicians,students alumni,benefactors,
and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university Community.It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching,research,and professional development.

This year's Convocation has selected the topic "The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development in Tanzania" as the standing point of the reflect to the theme of this year's 18 November African statistics day.The theme state that " Modernising data ecosystem to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area(AfCTA):The role of official statistics and big data in economict transformation and sustainable development of Africa ".

African statistics day is celebrated each year on 18th of November.The commemoration aims among others,to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development.This year's Convocation aims to understand the African Continental Free Trade Area(AFCFTA)and the potential role statistics and big data plays in discovering market and serving customers across Africa.
